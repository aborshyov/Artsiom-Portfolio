AthleteOS Case Study

From athlete experience to career-ready language.

Problem

Athletes have strong experience, but it is hard to explain it to employers.

Many athletes have leadership, discipline, pressure, teamwork, and performance habits, but those strengths often do not translate clearly into resumes, job searches, or outreach.

What I Built

A live AI product that turns athletic background into career direction.

AthleteOS creates career-path ideas, resume bullets, job-fit insights, and LinkedIn outreach language for NCAA, professional, international, and former athletes.

Tools Used

Next.js, Tailwind, GitHub, Vercel, and AI workflow design.

I built the MVP with a modern web stack, structured prompts, clean user flows, demo athlete data, and a simple interface designed around fast career exploration.

Result

A deployed product that proves AI product thinking and business judgment.

The project shows my ability to identify a real user problem, design the workflow, build the product, deploy it publicly, and communicate the value clearly.
